Elise Grosjean

postdoctoral researcher

Felix-Klein-Zentrum für Mathematik

I am a postdoctoral researcher in applied mathematics at Felix-Klein-Zentrum für Mathematik.

My research interests include fluid mechanics, P.D.E analysis, numerical schemes, and reduced order methods.

Interests
  • Finite elements/volume methods
  • Computational Langages
  • Function approximations
  • Reduced Order methods

Implementation of a DG 3D problem for meniscus regeneration
Felix-Klein-Institut für Mathematik Mar 2022
Biomedical problem: Meniscus regeneration, cell and tissue-level phenomena.

** Sensitivity analysis of the parameters with model order reduction techniques
